International Resources and Translators

This page provides contact information for volunteers who have offered to provide translation services for FDS and Smokeview support and documentation. Some have made translations of the documents provided by NIST. Links to the translated documents can be found below. Please note that we are not able to review these documents and do not make any claims regarding the accuracy of this information. If there are any errors in the documents, please notify the associated contact.
