Introduction

Fire Dynamics Simulator (FDS) is a computational fluid dynamics (CFD) model of fire-driven fluid flow. The software solves numerically a form of the Navier-Stokes equations appropriate for low-speed, thermally-driven flow, with an emphasis on smoke and heat transport from fires.

Smokeview (SMV) is an OpenGL visualization program that is used to display the output of FDS and CFAST simulations.

The Fire Dynamics Simulator and Smokeview applications are developed by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) of the United States Department of Commerce, in cooperation with VTT Technical Research Centre of Finland.

FDS Users E-Mail List and FDS-SMV Discussion Group

If you would like to be notified of news and information regarding FDS and Smokeview, please register by sending an email to, listproc@nist.gov.
The body of the message should contain the phrase:
subscribe fdsusers Jane Doe
Where you MUST replace the words Jane Doe with your first and last name.
NOTE: The subject line may be left blank.

To unsubscribe from the list send an email to the same address containing the line unsubscribe fdsusers
Past postings of the FDS/Smokeview mailing list can be found in the Listserv Archive.
You cannot reply to the mailing list messages.

To participate in the FDS-SMV Discussion Group, you will need to subscribe to the Group as a member first on the Group Site. You can sign up here:
http://groups.google.com/group/fds-smv/subscribe
Once you are a member of the group, you can also send email directly from your registered email address to the group at, fds-smv@googlegroups.com
